8th FEBRUARY 1922
From Resident Officer Onitsha Province
To the Lieutenant Governor at Enugu

Correspondence: Petition from Chiefs Enugu District

Petition request from Certain chiefs of Enugu requesting Government to pay them an annual rent for the Coalfields and Timber reservation

The resident officer held a meeting to dispel the question of government paying rent or compensation because the government railway and coalfields provide job for the communities.

R.R Robert- Senior Resident Officer Onitsha Province
